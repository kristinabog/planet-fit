Our Services

Personal Training

With a team of over 20 trainers, we offer personal training with high quality and with the needed personal attention.

Group Fitness Classes

We offer different kind of classes which are suitable for any levels. These include: HIIT, Body pump, TRX, Core, Zumba and Cross HIT.

Pilates

Pilates is a method of exercise that consists of low-impact flexibility and muscular strength and endurance movements.

Spinning Classes

Our spinning classes are offered every day in the morning and evening, so everyone can join them.

Yoga Classes

Relax your body after a good workout and join our yoga classes which we offer often throughout the week.

Strength training

We have an amazing range of gym equipment. we have areas for cardio, free weights, weights machines and more!
