Our Services

 
Personal Training

With a team of over 20 trainers, we offer personal training with high quality and with the personal attention anyone needs to reach their goals.

 
Group Fitness Classes

We offer different kind of classes which are suitable for any levels. These include: HIIT, Body pump, Abmania Zumba, Kickboxing and Circuit training.

 
Pilates

Pilates is a method of exercise that consists of low-impact flexibility and muscular strength and endurance movements. We offer Pilates 3x a week, with a highly experienced trainer.

 
Spinning Classes

Our spinning classes are offered every day in the morning and evening, so everyone can join them.

 
Yoga Classes

Relax your body after a good workout and join our yoga classes which we offer every morning, afternoon and evening.

 
Strength training

We have an amazing range of gym equipment. we have areas for cardio, free weights, weights machines and more!
